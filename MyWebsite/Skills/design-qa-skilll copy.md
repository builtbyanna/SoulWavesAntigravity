# Skill: design-qa

## Purpose
Review a website like a senior creative developer and UX reviewer.

## Inputs Required
- Current website files or screenshots
- Optional live URL
- Business goal

## Steps

### Step 1: Review visual hierarchy
Check headings, section rhythm, CTA visibility, and content clarity.

### Step 2: Review design consistency
Check spacing, typography, colors, corner radius, shadows, and image treatment.

### Step 3: Review motion and polish
Check transitions, hover states, animation restraint, and scroll quality.

### Step 4: Review usability
Check navigation, readability, responsiveness, and friction points.

### Step 5: Prioritize
List:
- Critical
- Important
- Minor
- Positive observations

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Prioritized clearly
- [ ] Not just criticism
- [ ] Business context respected

## Output
A prioritized design/UX review.
