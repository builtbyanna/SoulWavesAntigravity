# Skill: scrollytelling-concept

## Purpose
Generate elegant scroll-driven section concepts that fit the business instead of feeling gimmicky.

## Inputs Required
- Business type
- Core transformation
- Desired mood
- Technical ambition level

## Steps

### Step 1: Clarify transformation
Identify what change the business creates.

### Step 2: Translate into visual metaphor
Generate 3 possible visual storytelling concepts.

### Step 3: Rank by complexity
For each concept, explain:
- business meaning
- scroll behavior
- implementation complexity
- performance risk
- mobile fallback

### Step 4: Recommend one
Choose the best fit based on elegance, clarity, and feasibility.

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Fits business type
- [ ] Not over-gimmicky
- [ ] Mobile fallback included
- [ ] Performance considered

## Output
Three concepts plus one recommended direction.
