# Skill: website-audit

## Purpose
Audit an in-progress or existing website for structure, visual quality, UX, SEO, and implementation weaknesses.

## Inputs Required
- Website files or current repo
- Optional screenshots or live URL
- Business goal of the site

## Steps

### Step 1: Understand the site
Identify site type, target audience, business goal, and current section structure.

### Step 2: Audit structure
Review information hierarchy, section order, CTA clarity, and conversion flow.

### Step 3: Audit design
Review typography, spacing, visual consistency, image quality, motion usage, and overall polish.

### Step 4: Audit UX and technical quality
Check responsiveness, accessibility concerns, performance risks, and structural issues.

### Step 5: Audit SEO basics
Check heading hierarchy, semantic structure, metadata gaps, alt text gaps, and internal linking opportunities.

### Step 6: Prioritize
List findings by severity:
- Critical
- Important
- Nice-to-have

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Clear explanation of what already works
- [ ] Clear explanation of what is weak
- [ ] Findings prioritized
- [ ] Recommendations fit the business type

## Output
A structured audit with strengths, weaknesses, and a prioritized action plan.
