## SERVICES

### "What does a typical project look like week by week?"

**Websites (custom-coded):**
- Week 1: Discovery call, scope definition, Anna looks at what exists (if anything). Proposal sent within 24-48h.
- Week 2: Design direction, structure, first build. Anna builds in Claude Code, shares progress via screenshots or staging link.
- Week 3-4: Revisions, content integration (client provides texts/images), mobile optimization, testing.
- Week 4-5: Go-live, handover, quick walkthrough if needed.
- Total: 3-5 weeks for a standard site.

**Websites (WordPress):**
- Same flow, but add plugin setup, theme configuration, and a short training so the client can edit content themselves.
- Total: 3-6 weeks depending on complexity.

**Course/Membership Platforms (WordPress):**
- Week 1-2: Discovery, platform decision, scope. Anna decides the tech stack based on what the client actually needs.
- Week 2-4: Build: payment system, access groups, video setup, login flow, email automations.
- Week 4-6: Content upload (client provides videos/texts), testing all purchase and access flows, mobile testing.
- Week 6-8: Launch support, documentation so the client can add new content independently.
- Total: 6-8 weeks. More complex than a website because payments, access control, and email flows all need to work together.
- Optional add-on: Anna can also edit the course videos (see Video Editing). This is scoped and priced separately.

**AI Chatbot (add-on):**
- 1-2 weeks. Anna builds the bot, trains it on the client's content, integrates it into the site, tests it.
- Ongoing: updates as the client's offers or info changes (included in retainer or billed separately).

**Video Editing:**
- Per-video basis. Client sends raw footage, Anna delivers edited video within 2-5 days depending on length and complexity.
- Full scope available: thumbnails, branded intro/outro, color and audio adjustments, cutting reels/short clips, captions, text overlays, sound effects.
- Scope is flexible: the client decides what they need. Some want the full package, some just want a clean cut. Anna adapts.
- Tools: DaVinci Resolve (primary), Canva (for reels, social clips, text overlays).

**Digital Operations Retainer:**
- Ongoing monthly. Anna handles a defined set of tasks (website updates, platform maintenance, video editing, chatbot updates) on a rolling basis. Communication via WhatsApp or email, updates proactively.

### "What do clients always misunderstand about each service?"

**Websites:** That "a website" is a fixed thing. Requirements always evolve during the build. Early scoping saves money.

**Course Platforms:** That "I just need a video platform" is simple. It's actually six problems stacked on top of each other: payments, access control, video hosting, login, email flows, and mobile experience.

**AI Chatbot:** That it replaces human contact. It handles the repetitive questions and captures leads. It doesn't replace a real conversation.

**Video Editing:** That editing is just "cutting." It's also pacing, color, audio, thumbnails, branding consistency.

**Retainer:** That it's "on call." It's a defined monthly scope, not unlimited availability.

### "What Anna does NOT do within each service"

**Websites:** No copywriting from scratch (Anna shapes it together with the client, but the client provides the core content. If the client needs a copywriter, Anna can connect them with trusted copywriters in her network). No stock photography sourcing. No SEO campaigns or ongoing marketing. No complex e-commerce (that's a separate project). Anna can create custom visual assets using Midjourney for clients who need original imagery.

**Course Platforms:** No community management, no student engagement. Anna builds the technical layer and hands over the keys. The client runs the business. Anna can advise on course structure if helpful, but content strategy decisions stay with the client.

**AI Chatbot:** No ongoing content writing for the bot. Anna builds and trains it; the client provides the knowledge. No integration with external CRM systems (unless specifically scoped).

**Video Editing:** No filming. No scriptwriting. No complex motion graphics. Clean, functional editing. Scope is always tailored to what the client needs.

**Retainer:** No social media management. No strategic consulting. No client communication on behalf of the client. The retainer covers technical and digital tasks only.

**General VA services:** If a client specifically asks for inbox management, calendar coordination, or administrative work, Anna is open to discussing it. These aren't core services but can be arranged case by case.

**Project management:** Anna prefers using Notion for project collaboration when the client is open to it. She sets up a shared workspace where both sides can track progress, share files, and communicate about the project.

## PROCESS

### How does a new project start?

1. **Discovery call** (free, 20-30 minutes): Anna listens to what the client needs, asks questions, figures out the right approach. No selling, no pressure.
2. **Proposal** (within 24-48 hours): A clear document with exactly what Anna will build, the timeline, the price, and what the client needs to provide.
3. **Client confirms**: Scope may adjust slightly between call and written agreement. That's normal.
4. **Contract + first invoice** sent together in one email. Work starts after signature and payment.
5. **Kick-off call**: Only after contract is signed and first payment received. This is where the actual build planning happens.

### What does Anna need from the client before starting?

- Access to existing accounts (hosting, domain, platform logins) if applicable
- Content: texts, images, logos, branding guidelines (whatever exists)
- A clear idea of what the business offers and who it's for (Anna helps shape this in the discovery call if needed)
- Responsiveness: when Anna asks a question or sends something for review, a timely answer keeps the project on track

**What Anna does NOT need:** The client doesn't need to understand tech, know which platform is right, or have everything perfectly prepared. That's Anna's job.

### What's the typical back-and-forth during a project?

- Communication via WhatsApp or email (whatever the client prefers)
- Anna sends progress updates proactively, doesn't wait to be asked
- 1-2 feedback rounds per major milestone
- If something is delayed or a bug comes up, Anna says so immediately, not after a week of silence

### What happens after launch?

**Option A: Handover.** Anna delivers the project, does a walkthrough (video or call), provides documentation if needed. Client takes over. Done.

**Option B: Hosting & maintenance retainer.** €50-100/month. Anna keeps the site live, handles small updates, manages hosting. For clients who don't want to think about tech after launch.

**Option C: Digital Operations Retainer.** €300-400/month. The full ongoing digital back-end: maintenance, updates, video editing, chatbot updates, visual assets, platform management. For clients who need ongoing support. For the full list of what's included, refer to the services described on the Soul Waves website.

Anna always recommends at least Option B after a website build. Websites need occasional updates, and it's cheaper to prevent problems than to fix them later.
