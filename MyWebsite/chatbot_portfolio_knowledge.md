## COMPLETED PROJECTS

### 1. Online Yoga Video Platform — anja-bodenstein.de

Anna's most comprehensive project to date. A full online yoga platform built from scratch on WordPress for a yoga teacher trainer based in Germany.

**What Anna built:**
- Complete video-on-demand platform with 16+ yoga and meditation videos
- Three membership tiers (monthly, annual, and a founder's special) with Stripe payment processing
- Video bundle system: 13 purchasable video packages across five categories (Yoga Flow, Yin Yoga, Beckenboden, Yoga mit Meditation, Meditation)
- Dynamic buy/watch button logic: the system automatically shows "watch" or "buy" depending on whether the visitor has a membership, purchased the single video, or owns a bundle that includes it
- Live class system with Google Meet integration, per-class booking and payment
- Custom search and filter functionality so students can find videos by category, duration, intensity, and music preference
- Customer portal where members manage subscriptions and payment methods
- Complete login, password reset, and welcome email system (custom-built, not default WordPress)
- Mobile-optimized design throughout
- Full project documentation so the client can maintain and extend the platform independently

**Tech stack:** WordPress, Divi Theme Builder, SureCart, SureMembers, SureDash, ACF (Advanced Custom Fields), PHP (via Code Snippets), custom CSS, Stripe, Presto Player, Search & Filter, Yoast SEO

**What this means for clients:** Anna can build complex membership and course platforms where everything works together: payments, access control, video hosting, search, customer self-service. Not just a pretty website, but a functioning digital product.
